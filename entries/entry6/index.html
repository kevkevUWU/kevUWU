Chilaquiles

What are Chilaquiles?

Chilaquiles are a popular Mexican breakfast dish made of crispy fried corn tortillas cut into quarters, topped with salsa, cheese, eggs, and sometimes chicken. They're often served with refried beans and avocado on the side.

Basic Chilaquiles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 corn tortillas, cut into wedges
  • 1 cup of salsa (red or green)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked shredded chicken (optional)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled cheese (queso fresco)
  • Sour cream, cilantro, avocado (for garnish)
