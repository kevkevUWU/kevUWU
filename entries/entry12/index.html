Breakfast Around the World!

Baozi

Or simply bao, is a type of yeast-leavened filled bun. There are many variations in fillings and preparations, though the buns are most often steamed.

Pho

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese soup featuring flavorful broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, typically beef or chicken.

Croissant

A Croissant is a flaky, buttery, crescent-shaped pastry made from laminated dough, originating in France, popular worldwide.

Pingado

Pingado is a traditional Brazilian drink made by mixing espresso with steamed milk, typically served sweetened and enjoyed at breakfast.

Idli

Idli is a soft, fluffy South Indian steamed rice and lentil cake, often enjoyed with sambar and coconut chutney.

Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles is a traditional Mexican dish featuring tortilla chips simmered in salsa, topped with cheese, cream, and optional protein.

Burger

A breakfast burger combines classic morning flavors like eggs, bacon, and cheese with a juicy patty, perfect for a hearty start.

Tartine

A tartine is an open-faced sandwich, typically featuring bread topped with spreads, cheeses, meats, or vegetables, often elegantly presented.

Pizza

Pizza is a baked flatbread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients, popular for its versatility and deliciousness.
