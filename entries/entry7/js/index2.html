Different Types of Burgers

Classic Cheeseburger

A timeless favorite made with a juicy beef patty, melted cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles, served on a toasted bun.

Veggie Burger

A plant-based delight featuring a patty made from a blend of vegetables, grains, and legumes, topped with fresh greens and vegan mayo.

Bacon Burger

A savory burger with crispy bacon strips, melted cheese, caramelized onions, and BBQ sauce, served on a toasted bun.

Chicken Burger

Grilled or crispy chicken breast, topped with lettuce, tomato, and mayo, served on a soft bun for a lighter alternative.

Portobello Mushroom Burger

A juicy grilled portobello mushroom cap, topped with melted cheese, arugula, and garlic aioli, perfect for vegetarians.

Spicy Jalapeño Burger

A fiery burger topped with jalapeños, pepper jack cheese, spicy mayo, and crispy onions for those who love the heat.
