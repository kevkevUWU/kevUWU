About Pingado

Pingado is a traditional Brazilian beverage made by mixing strong coffee with milk. It can be enjoyed as a breakfast drink or at any time of the day.

Recipe

  1. Prepare a cup of strong coffee.
  2. Heat the milk and froth it.
  3. Pour the hot milk into the coffee, adjusting the ratio to your taste.
  4. Add sugar or spices according to preference.

Benefits

  • Provides energy: Caffeine helps to energize.
  • Nutrient-rich: Milk is high in calcium and vitamins.
  • Unique flavor: The combination of coffee and milk creates a distinctive taste.
