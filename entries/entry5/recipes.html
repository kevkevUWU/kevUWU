Delicious Idli Recipes

Classic Idli

This traditional recipe uses fermented rice and urad dal. Steamed to perfection, these idlis are soft and fluffy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and dal separately for 6-8 hours.
  2. Grind dal and rice to a smooth batter, mix with salt.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight.
  4. Pour batter into idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.

Rawa Idli

A quick and easy version made with semolina (rava), ideal for breakfast or a snack!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Mix semolina, yogurt, salt, and water to make a thick batter.
  2. Add baking soda and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour batter into idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.

Vegetable Idli

Add some nutrition with this vegetable-packed version!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups idli batter
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare idli batter as per your choice.
  2. Mix in finely chopped vegetables and salt.
  3. Pour into idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.
